BA II Plus Calculator is a mobile application software based on BA II PLUS calculator. Its use method is consistent with that of physical calculator, which is convenient for CFA/FRM candidates to learn and use.

  • Time Value of Money calculations.
  • Amortization schedules.
  • Cash flow analysis with NPV and IRR.
  • Mortgage and Loan calculations.
  • Options to turn on/off key click feedback sound, and
  • Statistical analysis.
  • Scientific capabilities, including log, sin, cos etc.
  • Amortization schedules.
  • Bond prices and yields “to maturity” or “to call” calculations, and Modified Duration (DUR).
  • Choice of Chain and Algebraic Operating System methods.

Both iPhone and iPad versions included in one application
